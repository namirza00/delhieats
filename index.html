OUR STORY

It all began when ......

Mr.Desai started to miss food from back home. He searched everywhere, but he was unable to find authentic flavors and staple dishes such as the ones found in the streets of New Delhi.So, he decided to do it himself by opening Delhi Eats in the spring of 1994. His vision was to share a slice of Delhi's culinary delights with his fellow New Yorkers. After years of hard labor of love, Delhi Eats stands as one of the most outstanding restaurant in New York City and has high reviews from Yelp and Zagat.

Our Way: 3 Main Principles

What's seperates us from the crowd are our 3 main principles:

  1. Original Delhi family recipes prepared by our well-esteemed chefs
  2. Daily fresh produce brought in by our local independent suppliers
  3. Treating customers with the best quality of service
